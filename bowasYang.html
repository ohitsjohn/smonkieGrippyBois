Bowas Yang

Camera, Director of Photography

AC

Full Loadout

Bowas works primarily as a camera assistant. He has worked as an assistant for countless student projects as well as over 7 short films. He has over a hundred different tools for maintaining the camera department and his extensive set experience will allow him to customize his kit for any situation on set from below freezing temperatures to blistering summer heat.

DP

Shock Value

Bowas works as a DP, having currently holding 6 DP credits for short films. He specializes in creating dark atmospheres, splashes of colored neon lights, and has an affinity towards the gritty and violent.

Camera Operator

Dynamics

Bowas also works as a camera operator. Usually on smaller projects, Bowas will operate while holding the mantle of DP. He specializes in studio camera operations and is familiar with RED, ARRI, Canon, and Sony cinema cameras as well as smaller consumer cameras.

Grip

Jury Rigged

Bowas can also integrate with JCOGs as a grip, similar to the other JCOG members. He boasts of his explosive strength and quick thinking to create efficient rigs that work well while being efficient.
